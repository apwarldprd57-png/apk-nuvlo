Privacy Policy for Nuvlo

Effective Date: 25 June 2025
Last Updated: 27 August 2025

Nuvlo is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is collected and used properly, lawfully, and transparently. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application ("Nuvlo").


---1. Information We Collect---
Nuvlo prioritizes privacy and minimal data collection. We do *not* collect your name, phone number, email address, or physical address. Instead, we generate a unique identifier based on.
- Device information(model, Ip Address, screen size, time zone)
- IP address (for limited use only)
- App activity (posts, likes, reports)
- Chosen username and password (encrypted)
- Reset PIN (generated for you)


---2. How We Use Your Information---
We use the limited data we collect for the following:
- Account authentication
- Content moderation
- Restricting abuse (e.g., spam, multiple account creation)
- Improving app performance and content discovery
- Investigating reports of harmful or illegal content
- Ensuring security and device-based account integrity


---3. Your Privacy Rights---
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your data
- Restrict or object to certain processing
- Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

We honor these rights where applicable under:
- POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act – South Africa)
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation – EU)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act – USA)

--You can request account deletion directly from the settings.



---4. Data Storage and Security---
Your data is securely stored in our datsbase, a trusted cloud infrastructure. Security measures include: - Encrypted password and reset PIN storage - Device-level authentication We do *not* sell or share user data with third parties.



---5. User Reporting & Harmful Content---
Users can report harmful content. While Nuvlo operates without real-time moderation, we are committed to:
- Reviewing reports regularly
- Banning devices linked to violations (max 3 accounts per device)
- Maintaining a safe, respectful environment
We are not liable for content shared by users but will act promptly when notified of violations.



---6. Children’s Privacy--- Nuvlo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware of such data, it will be deleted immediately.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified through the app. Continued use of Nuvlo after changes means you accept the revised policy.
